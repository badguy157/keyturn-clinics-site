Past work outside clinics

Hospitality project

The Anchor Hotel, Haydon Bridge

A riverside hotel in England. The old site made it hard for guests to book. Too many left to use travel sites like Booking.com. We rebuilt the site around booking and clear info. Now more guests book direct.

View the live site
Click to enlarge →

Before → What Changed → Results

Result: Direct bookings went from single digits to about 25 percent share in the first year.

Before

The old site had several problems that sent guests to booking portals instead.

  • • Buried booking button hard to find
  • • Slow mobile pages that frustrated visitors
  • • Unclear room information and pricing
  • • No tracking to see what was working

What Changed

We rebuilt the site around booking and trust.

  • • Rebuilt the site around direct bookings
  • • Added clear "Book a room" buttons on every page
  • • Created simple pages for rooms and local area
  • • Optimized for fast mobile experience

Results

The results showed up quickly and stayed consistent.

  • • Direct bookings rose from 8% to 25% (+17 percentage points)
  • • Google clicks increased by ~107%
  • • More calls and email inquiries

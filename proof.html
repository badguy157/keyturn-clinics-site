Quick answers

Are these real clinics or demos?

The dermatology, cosmetic surgery, and medspa mockups shown above are fictional demo builds created to demonstrate booking-focused layouts. The Anchor Hotel case study is real work done outside the clinic space, included to show our conversion-focused approach.

Can you show examples for my specialty?

Yes. We have demo mockups for dermatology, cosmetic surgery, medspa, cosmetic dental, and plastic surgery shown on this page. Each demonstrates how we structure patient-flow focused sites for different clinic types.

What results should I expect?

Most clinics see a 15-40% increase in booked consults from the same traffic. Results vary by specialty, current site performance, and traffic volume. The Free Scan will give you a baseline to measure against.

What's the usual timeline?

Typical rebuild takes 3-4 weeks from kickoff to launch. This includes design mockups, development, your review rounds, and final deployment. Rush timelines available if needed.

Past work outside clinics

Non-clinic case study (real project)

Hospitality project

The Anchor Hotel, Haydon Bridge

A riverside hotel in England. The old site made it hard for guests to book. Too many left to use travel sites like Booking.com. We rebuilt the site around booking and clear info. Now more guests book direct.

View the live site
Click to enlarge →

Before → What Changed → Results

Result: Direct bookings went from single digits to about 25 percent share in the first year.

Before

The old site had several problems that sent guests to booking portals instead.

  • • Buried booking button hard to find
  • • Slow mobile pages that frustrated visitors
  • • Unclear room information and pricing
  • • No tracking to see what was working

What Changed

We rebuilt the site around booking and trust.

  • • Rebuilt the site around direct bookings
  • • Added clear "Book a room" buttons on every page
  • • Created simple pages for rooms and local area
  • • Optimized for fast mobile experience

Results

The results showed up quickly and stayed consistent.

  • • Direct bookings rose from 8% to 25% (+17 percentage points)
  • • Google clicks increased by ~107%
  • • More calls and email inquiries

What was wrong before

The old site was hard to use on phones. Visitors couldn't see how to book. Too many guests went to travel sites and the hotel paid extra fees.

Our goals

  • Get more direct bookings.
  • Cut fees paid to online travel sites.
  • Make it easy to book on every page.
  • Make the site feel calm, clear, and modern.

What we changed

We rebuilt the site around booking and trust.

  • New home page with clear calls to book.
  • Simple pages for rooms, food, and local area.
  • Clear buttons to "Book a room" on every key page.
  • Better photos and simple copy.
  • Tracking set up to see what pages bring bookings.

This project followed the same process we use today: a short Clinic Booking Blueprint & Site Audit to find the main leaks, followed by a focused rebuild and a 90-day launch sprint to keep improving.

The money saved

Why this matters for owners.

Each booking moved from travel sites to direct saves about $20 to $25 per stay.

With hundreds of bookings per year, that adds up to thousands in savings.

Show the math

Saved per moved booking = $90.65 rate × 1.51 nights × 18% fee ≈ $24.60.

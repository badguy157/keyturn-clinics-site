Referral Thank-You for Clinics

Most of our work comes from word of mouth. If you like working with us and know another clinic that needs a better website, this page explains how we say thank you for the introduction.

Who this is for

This referral thank-you is for current and past Keyturn clients and close partners.

  • Medspas and aesthetic spas
  • Cosmetic and plastic surgery clinics
  • Cosmetic and implant-focused dental practices
  • Aesthetic dermatology clinics

How it works

  • You introduce another clinic or practice owner to us by email or message.
  • We have a short call or email thread with them and, if it's a fit, send a proposal.
  • If they sign a Core, Pro, or Premier website project and pay the initial deposit, your referral is counted as successful.

Your thank-you

For each successful referral, you can choose one thank-you:

  • $500 cash
  • or $750 in Keyturn credit that you can use toward a Growth Sprint, extra landing pages, or other future work.

How to refer someone

Referring someone is simple. You can:

  • Send a short intro email connecting us with the clinic owner or practice manager.
  • Or ask us for a forwardable email that you can send to your colleague with a link to this site.

When you make an intro, please mention that you'd like it tracked as a referral so we can make sure your thank-you is recorded.

A quick note on ethics

This program is only for referrals to other clinics or service businesses that might need a website rebuild. It is not for referring individual patients or cases.

We do not pay for patient referrals or anything related to clinical decision-making. This is simply a way to say thank you when you introduce another clinic that chooses to work with us on their website.

Have a clinic in mind? Email hello@keyturn.studio and we'll send you a short intro email you can forward.

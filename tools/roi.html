About this estimate
  • This is a rough estimate, not a guarantee
  • Uses conservative inputs (extra patients/month)
  • Only counts incremental patients attributed to the website (not referrals, retention, or ads)
  • Real-world results vary by offer, traffic quality, and booking flow

How to read this number

  • This is a rough estimate, not a guarantee.
  • We keep the math conservative. Many clinics see more upside over time.
  • The calculator only looks at new patients from the website, not all of your marketing.
  • If your site already gets strong traffic, even small lifts in bookings can add up fast.
  • < 6 months: Very strong upside. You are likely underpriced or underbuilt.
  • 6–12 months: Healthy. A rebuild can make sense if you want growth.
  • 12+ months: Slow. It may still be worth it for brand and trust, but we should talk through your numbers.

Note: For Premier projects we use the minimum package price in the calculation. Actual Premier pricing varies based on scope.

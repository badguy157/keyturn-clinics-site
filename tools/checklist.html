15-point clinic website checklist for patient-flow

Use this quick checklist to see if your clinic or medspa site is set up to turn visitors into booked patients.

Takes about 5 minutes to go through.

How to use this checklist

  • Give yourself 1 point for every item you can honestly say "yes" to.
  • Be strict. If it is half true, treat it as a "no".
  • Add up your score at the end to see where you stand.

Hero & First Impression

  1. Clear, patient-friendly headline that says who you help and what you do
  2. Visible "Book a visit" or "Request a consult" buttons on both desktop and mobile
  3. High-quality photo or visual that conveys professionalism and trust

Treatments & Services

  1. Dedicated pages for your highest-value treatments
  2. Clear descriptions that explain benefits, not just procedures
  3. Pricing indicators or starting costs where appropriate

Booking & Conversion

  1. Easy-to-find booking link or button on every page
  2. Working contact form that sends emails reliably
  3. Clear next steps after someone fills out a form or makes a request

Trust & Credibility

  1. Patient testimonials or reviews visible on the homepage or key pages
  2. Before/after photos (where appropriate and compliant)
  3. Provider credentials, certifications, or about section

Mobile Experience

  1. Website loads quickly and looks good on mobile devices
  2. Tap-to-call button clearly visible on mobile
  3. Forms are easy to fill out on a phone screen

What your score means

0–7 points: Your site is leaking bookings. A rebuild or big refresh will help a lot.

8–12 points: You are doing some things right, but there is room to grow bookings.

13–15 points: You are in good shape. Small tweaks and better traffic can go far.

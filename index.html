Common leaks we see

  • • Broken forms or dead links.
  • • No clear "Book now" step on mobile.
  • • High-value treatments buried under long menus.
  • • No proof (photos or reviews) near your booking buttons.

What we do instead

  • ✓ Make the booking path clear on phones first.
  • ✓ Show your best treatments and offers above the fold.
  • ✓ Add proof where patients decide to book or call.
  • ✓ Track what's working so you're not guessing.

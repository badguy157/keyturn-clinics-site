Common questions

Do you work with our existing booking or scheduling system?

In most cases, yes. We work with what you already use—whether that's an online booking tool, scheduling software, or a booking engine. As long as it can provide a direct booking link or widget, we can build your site around it and set up proper tracking.

How long does a project take?

Typically about 3 weeks once we have everything we need from you (brand assets, booking system details, and access to your domain/analytics).

Do you need access to our medical records system?

No—we only work with your public website and marketing tools. We never need or request access to patient records, clinical systems, or any confidential data.

What if we're not very technical?

That's fine. Most of our clients aren't technical. We handle all the tech work—you just need to approve content and make decisions about how you want to present your services.

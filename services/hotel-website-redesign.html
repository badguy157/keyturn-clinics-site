Hotel Website Redesign

Refresh your hotel website with a modern, conversion-focused redesign. Faster, mobile-friendly, and built to drive direct bookings.

Signs you need a redesign

  • Slow load times: Guests bounce before seeing your rooms
  • Poor mobile experience: Hard to navigate on phones (where most bookings start)
  • Weak booking call-to-action (CTA) buttons: Unclear path to book directly
  • No tracking: Can't measure which pages or campaigns drive bookings
  • Hard to update: Every change requires a developer
  • Low search rankings: Missing SEO fundamentals and schema markup

What we rebuild

  • Modern tech stack (fast, secure, maintainable)
  • Mobile-first design with clear booking flow
  • GA4 event tracking for bookings and key actions
  • JSON-LD schema for better search presence

  • Digital Asset Pack so you own everything

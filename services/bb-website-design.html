Questions B&B owners ask

Do I need a booking engine?

Not required. We can link to Booking.com, eviivo, or a contact form. Direct booking engines reduce fees, but we work with what you have.

Can you write content about my B&B?

Yes, in Pro tier. We'll interview you and craft copywriting that highlights your story, breakfast, and local attractions.

How much does it cost?

Core package starts at $7,500 (or $1,875/mo × 4). Includes home, rooms, about pages, booking call-to-action buttons, and GA4 tracking.

What if I want to add a gift shop or event space later?

Easy. Our Care plan includes updates and new pages. Or you can add them yourself because we provide full documentation.

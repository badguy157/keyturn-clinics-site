Pricing FAQs

Quick answers to common questions about pricing and process.

How do I know which package is right for me?

Book the 15-min Fit Check. We'll review your current site and recommend the best fit (whether that's Core, Pro, Premier, or just starting with the Blueprint). We'll tell you honestly if you should start smaller, bigger, or not at all.

What's the difference between a scan, Blueprint, and a full rebuild?

Free Scan: Quick surface-level analysis (60 seconds) showing your top leaks.
Blueprint ($1,000): Human-created plan with exact recommendations, sitemap, and booking flow strategy (credited if you rebuild within 90 days).
Full Rebuild: Complete website transformation (Core/Pro/Premier) built and launched in 3–5 weeks.

How long does a rebuild take?

Most projects go live in 3–5 weeks. Core typically takes 3–4 weeks, Pro takes 3–5 weeks, and Premier may take 4–6 weeks depending on complexity.

How does payment work?

Standard structure is 50% to start, 50% before launch, spread across 3–5 milestones depending on package size. All rebuilds start with the Clinic Booking Blueprint ($1,000), which is credited toward your package if we rebuild within 90 days. Need a different schedule? Just ask.

What do I need to provide?

You'll need: access to your online booking system or calendar, photos and logos, basic content about your services, and availability to provide feedback during 1-2 review checkpoints. We handle the rest (strategy, design, copy, and launch).

What happens after the Fit Check?

After the 15-min call, we'll send you a tailored package recommendation with exact quote, timeline, and what's included. If you proceed, we start with the Blueprint phase to map out your exact plan, then move into the build phase with regular review checkpoints.

How It Works

A clear 3-week sprint from first call to launch.

Most clinic projects take about 3 weeks once we have everything we need

We keep the process clear, organised, and low-stress. You get a website that works properly for patients — and you don't have to manage the tech side or spend weeks in back-and-forth.

The Process (Week 0–3)

Step 1: Request a quote & quick review

We review your current site and booking/scheduling setup, then confirm scope and timeline. You'll get a clear, one-page quote with recommendations in 1 business day.

Step 2: Discovery & access (Week 0–1)

We gather your goals, access to domain/CMS/booking tools, brand assets, and any must-have content. One kickoff call or email exchange, then we handle the setup.

Step 3: Structure, copy, and design (Week 1–2)

We map the patient flow, wireframe key pages, write clinic-friendly copy, and design the layout. You'll see drafts as we go and can request changes before we lock it in.

Step 4: Build, test, and launch (Week 2–3)

We implement the site, connect forms/call tracking, test on mobile and desktop, then launch. You get a staging link to review before we go live.

Step 5: Care & Growth (optional)

Month-to-month optimisation after launch. Choose Care ($300/mo) for maintenance and small updates, or Growth ($900/mo) for ongoing improvements and testing.

What we need from you

  • Access to domain / existing website platform (if any) and scheduling/booking tools.
  • Logo, brand colours, any good photos. We can work with what you have or guide you on what to source.
  • One primary contact who can answer questions and approve changes quickly.

We'll send you a simple checklist at kickoff so you know exactly what to prepare.

What you can expect from us

  • Clear project kickoff email with timeline, next steps, and what we need from you.
  • Weekly progress updates so you always know where we are and what's coming next.
  • Staging link before launch so you can review and approve the site before it goes live.
  • A simple launch checklist covering forms, call links, tracking, and any final details.

Security & data

  • We only work with the public website and marketing tools. We do not access medical records, PHI, or clinical systems.
  • You own your domain, site, and content. We hand everything over at the end in a Digital Asset Pack.
  • No patient data resale. We use simple analytics for tracking; no third-party pixels that sell patient information.

Your patient data stays private and secure. We're only here to help people find and book with you.

After launch

Care ($300/mo)

Safe hosting, backups, and software updates. Small text and image changes as needed. Basic checks that enquiry and booking journeys are working as expected.

Think of it as keeping the lights on and the doors open.

Growth ($900/mo)

Everything in Care, plus one meaningful website or funnel improvement each month — new blocks, layout tweaks, campaign landing pages, or A/B tests. Simple monthly email with the key numbers and what we changed.

For clinics that want to keep optimising and testing new approaches.

Both plans are month-to-month. Cancel anytime with 30 days' notice.

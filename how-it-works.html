The build process

Here's what happens from first chat to launch.

1

Get a quote

  • We review your current site and booking setup.
  • You get a one-page quote within 1 business day.
  • No pressure. Ask questions anytime.
2

Blueprint & kickoff

We start by turning your intake form and current site into a simple Blueprint.
• Page list and booking flow
• Key messages and call-to-actions
• Rough plan for the first version of the site

Then we walk through it together on a short call and lock in the plan. If you already did a paid Blueprint, we reuse that and just confirm it on the call.

3

Build & first review

We build the site using your Blueprint as the guide.

  • Small team builds your site: designer, writer, developer.
  • You get a private staging link to test on phone and desktop.
  • Simple feedback form for your changes.
  • This is revision round 1.
4

Final review & launch

  • We apply your feedback and polish the site.
  • You review the updated test link.
  • Revision round 2 via feedback form.
  • Once approved, we launch your new site.
5

Care & Growth (optional)

  • Care: 12-month term $300/mo (month-to-month $400/mo). Safe hosting, backups, small updates.
  • Growth: 12-month term $900/mo (month-to-month $1,100/mo). Everything in Care, plus monthly improvements.
  • Month-to-month plans can be cancelled any time with 30 days' notice.

How we work together

So you know exactly what we need, what we'll do, and how we keep things safe.

What we need from you

  • Access to your domain and booking tool
  • Completed intake form so we have your clinic details and goals
  • One main contact for answers and approvals
  • Quick responses during review rounds

What you can expect from us

  • Clear plan and timeline upfront
  • Weekly email updates on progress
  • Private test link before launch
  • Two revision rounds with feedback forms
  • Fast fixes in the first 30 days after launch

Security and data

  • We only work with your public website and booking tools
  • We never touch medical records or clinical systems
  • You own your domain, website, and content
  • No patient data is sold
  • Simple analytics only to see what works

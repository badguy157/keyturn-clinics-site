How It Works

A clear 3–5 week process from start to launch.

Most projects take 3–5 weeks once we have what we need

We keep things simple and low-stress. You get a website that works for patients. We handle the tech.

The Process

1

Get a quote

We review your current site and booking setup. You get a clear, one-page quote in 1 business day.

2

Kickoff

We gather what we need: your goals, access to your domain and booking tools, and your logo and photos. One kickoff call or email, then we get to work.

3

Your website build team gets to work

Once we're clear on your goals, we spin up a small build team just for your clinic with a designer, copywriter, and builder. The designer maps the key pages so everything feels calm, clean, and premium. The copywriter rewrites the words so they are simple, clear, and patient friendly. The builder maps out your booking steps, designs the phone-first layout, and connects your booking tools so you can see drafts and request changes as we go.

4

Build, test, and launch

We build the site, set up tracking (forms, calls, analytics), and test on mobile and desktop. You review on a test link before we go live.

5

Care and Growth (optional)

Ongoing support after launch. Choose Care ($300/mo) for maintenance, or Growth ($900/mo) for improvements and testing.

What we need from you

  • Access to your domain (and existing website if you have one) and your booking tool.
  • Logo, brand colors, and any good photos. We can work with what you have.
  • One main contact who can answer questions and approve changes.

We'll send you a simple checklist during onboarding so you know what to prepare.

What you can expect from us

  • Clear onboarding email with timeline, next steps, and what we need.
  • Weekly updates so you know where we are.
  • Test link before launch so you can review before we go live.
  • Simple launch checklist covering forms, call links, and tracking.
  • A small team handling strategy, design, building, and writing – you don't have to write pages yourself.

Security and data

  • We only work with your public website and marketing tools. We never touch medical records or clinical systems.
  • You own your domain, site, and content. We hand everything over at the end.
  • No patient data is sold. We use simple analytics for tracking. No third-party pixels that sell patient info.

Your patient data stays private. We just help people find and book with you.

After launch

Every project includes a 30-day launch warranty: we fix bugs and handle small text edits and simple photo swaps for the site we just launched at no extra charge. After that, ongoing changes and improvements are handled through a Care or Growth plan.

Care ($300/mo)

Safe hosting, backups, and updates. Small text and image changes as needed. We check that forms and booking steps work.

Think of it as keeping the lights on.

Growth ($900/mo)

Everything in Care, plus one real improvement each month (new section, new page, or layout tweak). Simple tests to help more visitors book. Monthly email with key numbers and what we changed.

For clinics that want to keep improving.

Both plans are month-to-month. Cancel anytime with 30 days' notice.

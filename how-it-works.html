The build process

Here's what happens from quote to launch.

1

Get a quote

We look at your current site and tell you what we'd do.

  • We look at your current site and booking setup.
  • You get a one-page quote within 1 business day.
  • No pressure. Ask questions anytime.
2

Kickoff

We gather what we need, then get to work.

  • We collect your logo, photos, and brand colors.
  • You give us access to your domain and booking tool.
  • One short call or email to go over your goals.
  • Then we start building.
3

Build & first review

We build your site. You review it on a test link.

  • A small team works on your site: designer, writer, builder.
  • You get a private test link to click through on phone and desktop.
  • We send a simple feedback form. You tell us what to change.
  • This is revision round 1.
4

Final review & launch

We make your changes, test everything, then go live.

  • We apply your feedback and polish the site.
  • You review the updated test link.
  • We send a second feedback form. This is revision round 2.
  • Once approved, we launch your new site.
5

Care & Growth (optional)

Ongoing help after launch if you want it.

  • Care ($300/mo): Hosting, backups, small updates.
  • Growth ($900/mo): Everything in Care, plus monthly improvements.
  • Month-to-month. No long contracts.

What we need from you

We keep the list short so your team can stay focused.

  • Access to your domain and booking tool. We need login info or admin access to set things up.
  • Logo, brand colors, and your best photos. We'll work with what you have. If you need new photos, we can help you plan.
  • One main contact for answers and approvals. This person reviews the site and gives us feedback at each round.

What you can expect from us

We keep you in the loop and make the process easy.

  • Clear plan and timeline. You know what's happening and when.
  • Weekly email updates. Short notes so you always know where we are.
  • Private test link. Click through your new site on phone and desktop before it goes live.
  • Two revision rounds with a feedback form. Easy way to tell us what to change.
  • We don't disappear after launch. We fix issues fast in the first 30 days.

Security and data

Your patients' trust matters. Here's how we keep things safe.

  • We only work with your public website and booking tools.
  • We never touch medical records or clinical systems.
  • You own your domain, website, and content.
  • No patient data is sold. We only use simple analytics to see what works.

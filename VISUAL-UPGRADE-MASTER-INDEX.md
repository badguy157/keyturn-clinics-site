## ğŸ“ Asset Specifications

### Images
- **Format:** JPG for photos, PNG for graphics with transparency
- **Dimensions:** 1920x1080px minimum (hero), 1200x800px (general)
- **File Size:** Under 500KB for JPG, under 300KB for PNG
- **Quality:** 80-90% compression

### Videos
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (desktop), 1280x720 (mobile)
- **File Size:** Under 5MB
- **Duration:** 10-20 seconds for loops
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps

### Icons
- **Format:** SVG
- **Size:** 24x24px (standard), 20x20px (small)
- **Color:** CurrentColor (inherits from CSS)

### Animations
- **Format:** JSON (Lottie)
- **File Size:** 10-100KB typical
- **Format:** Lottie JSON from LottieFiles

Welcome! Here's what happens next.

This page is only for new clients.
It explains what we'll do, what we need from you, and how we'll keep in touch.
It's okay to come back to this page any time.

What happens next

1. Kickoff

We review your current site and setup.

We have a short call to learn about your clinic.

We set up your project and start gathering what we need.

2. Design, build, and write

We map the booking steps and key pages.

We design the layouts and write first draft copy.

You see your site on a private test link.

3. Review, launch, and handover

You give feedback in two rounds of changes.

We test on phone and desktop, connect forms and tracking.

We launch the site and start your 30-day warranty.

Most projects take 3–5 weeks once we have what we need.

What we need from you

The essentials

With these items, we can start building right away.

  • Your completed intake form.
  • Domain or DNS access (or contact for the person who handles it).
  • Any logo, brand colors, and good photos you already have.
  • One main contact on your side for questions and approvals.

We'll send you a simple checklist as part of onboarding.

Helpful extras (can come later)

These help us do deeper work, but they are not required to start.

  • Access to your current website platform (if we're replacing or improving it).
  • Access to your booking or scheduling system (only if we need to connect forms).
  • Access to your analytics (like Google Analytics or Tag Manager). If you don't have this yet, we can help set it up.

Security note

We never ask for medical records, EMR, or patient data.

We only use logins for your website, domain, and marketing tools.

Use a password manager or our secure link instead of plain email.

What happens after you send this

  1. We confirm we have everything we need.
  2. We book your project into our schedule and share your target launch week.
  3. We send a short email or 20–30 min kickoff call link so you can ask questions.
  4. We share your private test link once the first version is ready.

You get two rounds of revisions before launch, using a simple feedback log we send you.

After launch, you have a 30-day warranty for bug fixes and small text or photo swaps at no extra charge.

How to share files and access securely

Here's how to share files and logins in a safe way.

  • Brand assets (logo, photos) – upload to a shared folder (Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive).
  • Website / domain access – invite our studio email as a user, or share via your password manager.
  • Analytics and booking tools – we'll send step-by-step instructions.

For more details, see our Security page.

How we keep you updated

We don't want you guessing what's going on.

  • Weekly email with progress and next steps.
  • Most questions answered within 1 business day.
  • We tell you when we need your feedback so the project keeps moving.

Quick questions

How many rounds of changes do we get before launch?

You get two rounds of design changes before launch. We send you a feedback log to fill in each time. After launch, you still have a 30-day warranty for small fixes and text or photo swaps.

What if we don't have all our photos ready?

No problem. We can start with the photos you have and use simple placeholders where needed. We'll swap in final photos during your revision rounds or within the 30-day warranty.

Can we change pages or wording later?

Yes. You'll be able to change simple text and photos yourself. For bigger layout or new pages, you can ask us for a quote or use a Care / Growth plan if you choose one.

What if we need to pause the project?

If you need to pause, just tell us as soon as you can. We'll agree on a new target date and explain any impact on timing.

How do we ask for small fixes after launch?

During the first 30 days after launch, email us any bugs or small text/photo changes. We'll group them and fix them at no extra charge. After 30 days, these are covered under Care / Growth plans or one-off support.

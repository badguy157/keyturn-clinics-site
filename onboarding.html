Welcome! Here's what happens next.

Some clinics start with a Clinic Booking Blueprint & Site Audit. Others go straight into a full build. This page is for new clients who have decided to move ahead with a rebuild. It tells you what we'll do, what we need from you, and how we'll stay in touch.

What happens next

1. Kickoff

We review your current site and confirm what we'll do. We'll have a short call to understand your clinic. Then we set up your project and start preparing.

2. Design, build, and write

We map out the booking steps, write the key pages, design the layouts, and start building. You can review progress on a test link.

3. Review, launch, and handover

We review the test site, make final changes, launch, and confirm tracking is working.

Most projects take 3 to 5 weeks once we have what we need.

What we need from you

With these essentials, we can start building right away.

We will never ask for passwords by email. We do not need access to medical records or patient data.

The essentials

With these items, we can start building right away.

  1. Your completed intake form. We will send you a short intake form. It gives us key details about your clinic and website goals. This helps us begin designing and building your new site.
  2. Domain or DNS access (or a contact for the person who handles it). We need access to your domain provider like GoDaddy, Namecheap, or Cloudflare. This lets us point your web address to the new site. You can use our secure link or ask your IT person to help.

Helpful extras (can be shared later)

These are optional and can be added any time during or after the project. They help us integrate more deeply or give you better insights, but they're not required to launch.

  • Logo, brand colors, and photos of your clinic, team, or rooms. These help the site feel more like you. We can start with whatever you have.
  • Access to your current website platform (only if we're editing an existing site instead of replacing it).
  • Access to your booking/scheduling system dashboard (only if you want us to adjust settings or integrate more deeply).
  • Access to your analytics like Google Analytics or Tag Manager. We use this to configure tracking and reporting. If you don't have analytics yet, we'll set it up for you.

What happens after you send this

  • Once we have access, we'll book your project and share your target launch week. Most projects take 3 to 5 weeks. We'll suggest a short kickoff call to walk through your priorities.
  • After kickoff we send you a website blueprint. Then we build your site. You get two review rounds on a private test link before launch.
  • If you're too busy for a call, we can handle the kickoff by email instead.
  • We'll also confirm if you want a Care or Growth plan after launch. This helps us keep improving results.
Tip: Want to avoid cookie pop-ups? Ask us about privacy-friendly analytics instead of Google Analytics.

How to share files and access securely

Use shared folders like Google Drive or Dropbox for photos. Use password managers for logins. Never send passwords by email.
